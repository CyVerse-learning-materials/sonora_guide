Sonora Discovery Environment Dashboard
---------------------------------------

The Dashboard provides an overview of recent activity inside CyVerse and the 
Discovery Environment that may be of interest to you. Here you can see
CyVerse-related news and events, video tutorials, and newly released public 
apps. Also, items specific to you will appear here, such as recently launched
analyses and newly integrated apps that you've been working on.

**Some of the sections available in the Dashboard**

- CyVerse News
- CyVerse Events
- CyVerse Videos
- Recent Analyses
- Recently Added Apps
- New Public Apps

Don't see a section that interests you? Let us know through our interactive 
support feature.
